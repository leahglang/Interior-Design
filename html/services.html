Budget planning

Interior design price | Preparing a budget for home renovation

The renovation budget is a particularly sensitive issue.
Like everyone else, we want everything and the best - and all this on a low renovation budget.
So how to estimate the cost of home renovation?

As mentioned, the renovation budget is a problematic and complex issue and there is no one right answer for everyone.

The cost of home renovation is derived from the costs of the professionals you choose.
Some of the Rays are more expensive and some are less.
Many times the price can also suggest to us different work qualities.

The renovation budget is also affected by choices made in favor of raw materials and finishes.

Every choice we make has an impact on the budget. Where will we invest and choose a high standard?
And where will we decide to "turn a blind eye" and choose a lower standard?

It is recommended that before you arrive at quotes for a contractor,
you start the renovation process with an interior planning and design service with an experienced and good interior designer.
A good interior designer will put together the best options for you under the budget definition.
This way you will have a minimum of deviations with the various professionals during the renovation.

architectural planning and drafting

Interior design | How do we plan the house?

In the first meeting we will take measurements and prepare a program while adapting to specific needs such as lifestyle habits,
favorite design style and get to know everything you need.

The second stage is the creation of sketches,
a smart and utilized division of the space and the placement of the furniture -
we will go over all the possible options for utilizing and dividing the area.

Another meeting where we will choose a favorite sketch or combine different sketches
into another one that will give you a perfect result.

In accordance with the chosen sketch - I will prepare a set of plans for execution,
plans intended for the contractor in the field, plans for demolition, construction, electricity, plumbing, flooring,
lighting, cladding layouts, lows, conditioning, and everything that is required.

Home styling | How to design the house?

Initial meeting, taking measurements, building a program and adjusting to specific needs.
(both in terms of lifestyle habits and in terms of preferred design style).

A meeting after visualization at a basic level to check the combination of materials and colors in the space
to check that I and the client are indeed on the same page and speak the same language
and that he is indeed satisfied with the result. In this meeting
I change the materials and colors in the space with the client himself,
so that at the end of the meeting we will exhaust all the options and come out with a selected one.

Preparation of detailed carpentry and paint layouts for a professional.
Sometimes special details are needed, so it's down to really small details like artisan carpentry or all kinds of special things.

Selection of materials - a meeting and going out to choose everything related to the chosen space.
Formicas, handles, surfaces, faucets, sanitary ware, lighting, accessories and complementary furniture.
