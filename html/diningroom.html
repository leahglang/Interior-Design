The house in Mount Shmuel

A four-story private house in Mount Shmuel surrounded by an ornamental garden.
The house is used by a family of seven members with different ages.
On the entrance floor there is a spacious bright and inviting living room
and a large, comfortable an well-equipped kitchen.
On the upper floors are the bedrooms and other rooms -
which allow each family member a private corner for their activities.
