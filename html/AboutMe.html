Nice to know you, I'm Tamar.

Interior and home designer - specializing in customization.

The motif that guides me in my work is improving the quality of life through proper planning
that integrates into the client's lifestyle and design adapted to his personal taste and needs.
I am a graduate of "Aman", the Center for Art and Design - the ultra-Orthodox
branch of "Bezalel" College - Jerusalem Academy of Art and Design.
I plan and design private and public spaces - apartments, houses, offices, shops and hotels
and accompany clients and projects from beginning to end, from planning to execution - from dream to reality.
